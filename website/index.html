About the Symposium

The Technical Symposium is organized every year by the Computer Science department to give students a platform to present academic papers, run hands-on workshops and take part in a hackathon judged by faculty and visiting industry professionals. This year's edition includes 12 tracks across AI, embedded systems, web development and cybersecurity, along with a 24-hour hackathon on the final two days.
